What types of eye conditions are checked during routine exams?

During routine eye exams at Eagle Vision Eye Care Clinic, the doctors check for a wide variety of common eye conditions. These include cataracts, which cause clouding of the lens and can lead to vision impairment if untreated; glaucoma, a condition that damages the optic nerve and can cause permanent vision loss; and macular degeneration, which affects the central part of the retina and is a leading cause of blindness in older adults. In addition to these conditions, the clinic checks for signs of diabetic retinopathy and other vision-related issues that may arise due to age, lifestyle, or underlying health conditions.

What technology does the clinic use for eye care?

Eagle Vision Eye Care Clinic prides itself on utilizing the latest cutting-edge technology for eye care. The clinic employs advanced diagnostic tools that can detect even the smallest changes in a patient’s eyes. These tools help the specialists identify eye health issues in their earliest stages, which is crucial for effective treatment. Technologies such as optical coherence tomography (OCT), retinal imaging, and computerized visual field testing are examples of the high-tech equipment used at the clinic.
